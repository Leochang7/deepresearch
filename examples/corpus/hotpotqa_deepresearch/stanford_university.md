# Stanford University

Stanford University, officially Leland Stanford Junior University, is a private research university in Stanford, California, United States. Founded in 1885 by Leland and Jane Stanford, it is one of the most prestigious universities in the world.

Stanford is located in the heart of Silicon Valley, approximately 35 miles southeast of San Francisco in Santa Clara County, California. The campus covers 8,180 acres, making it one of the largest university campuses in the United States.

The university has produced numerous notable alumni, including 83 Nobel laureates, 29 Turing Award recipients, and 8 Fields Medalists. Stanford has been closely associated with the development of Silicon Valley, with many technology companies founded by its alumni, including Google, Hewlett-Packard, and Yahoo.

Stanford University is located in the state of California, on the West Coast of the United States. It sits between the cities of Palo Alto and Menlo Park.
